The hidden secrets of water

Author: Dr. Paolo Consigli
Genre: science + mystery
Status:
Length: 164 pages

Brief description

In this book, readers will find themselves on adventures spanning millennia of water, through thousands of years old cultures, revisiting ancient theories such as Yin and Yang and Gaia, learn new theories such as the existence of apes - the ancestors of mankind...

Besides, the book also helps you better understand "living water" - the type of water commonly known as "alkaline ion hydrogen water", which has the effect of treating diseases that we often suffer from. to diet and environment.

Basically, the three things that make up life, diversity, intelligence include: water, free space in a cell or biomolecule, and a surface - boundary created by foreign matter (EZ Zones) ). Water is the common matrix of all growth, everything in nature is drawn with the invisible brush of water, forming an imperfect but extremely diverse and intelligent world. Water shapes and defines us as intelligent living beings on this planet.

The important role of water can be seen in nature and humans. However, our understanding of water is still likened to a "white belt" - a land that is not owned or controlled by anyone, not even scientific experts. Throughout the decades, studies of the true nature of water have continued to be carried out by physico-chemicals, yielding many surprising results.

Dr. Paolo Consigli not only brings the mysteries of water to light in the eyes of science. More importantly, through each chapter of his book about water, the Italian doctor clearly shows us the connection between water and life, water and the universe, water and man - from being a fetus to being a baby. an adult. From there, we understand the meaning of life hidden deep in the water, see our position between nature, between finite and infinite as well as the infinite potential of water.
